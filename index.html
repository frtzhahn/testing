What is Project Equal?

Project Equal is a digital encyclopedia designed by the researchers to provide accessible knowledge for all Senior High School students. In order to promote an equal access to education as well as maintaining the flexibility of the students' learning process, Project Equal was created to ensure a better quality of education. This project aims to contribute on solving at least 3 of the United Nations 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

Why is This Important?

Our goal is to improve students' understanding of different strands and make learning more flexible. By providing structured information, we aim to bridge the gap between different learning styles and needs. Our platform is designed to help students learn at their own pace and access information in a way that suits them best.

How Can You Use This?

Simply scan the QR codes or explore the webpage to access structured information from different strands. Our user-friendly interface makes it easy to find the information you need quickly and efficiently. You can access information on a wide range of topics, from history to science, and everything in between.

What Can You Expect?

Our platform is still at its development stage, but we researchers assure to you that this webpage will enable a more accessible education to all Grade 12 Students of Meycauayan National High School - Senior High that promotes students' flexibilty and equal learning and most of all less paper usage
