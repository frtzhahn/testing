What are the Specialized Subjects in Grade 12 ICT strand?

CSS also known as Computer System Servicing is the only Specialized subject for the Grade 12 ICT strand from 1st to 2nd Semester which explores topics from Certificate of Completion (COC) 1 to 4 that delves into a variety of topics mainly about setting up computers and many more in preparation for acquiring National Certificate 2 (NC II)

What to Expect?

You can find several forms of E-learning materials from this website such as:

- Video Lessons from the Department of Education Youtube Channel (Etulay)

- Downloadable documents and powerpoint presentations from Sir Jherald D. Esteban and Sir Elger R. David from COC 1 and 2
